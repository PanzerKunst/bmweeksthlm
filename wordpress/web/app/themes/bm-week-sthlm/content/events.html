### You are creating the schedule! As more events are created, they will be listed here on this web page. Event registration opened on Jan 4, so expect the schedule to start filling up within the next couple of weeks. Anyone can add their own events on the schedule, as long as you follow the [10 Principles](#ten-principles) and submit your application to our team. Learn more about [how to organize your own event](https://docs.google.com/a/guff.se/document/d/1Lo-5dj4rxQdQp6Xt0ByTapotJ3eAR8lzvmwKSjtT7aA/edit?usp=sharing) and [register it here for review](https://docs.google.com/a/guff.se/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfUU9K5wS8xoZn-BGfsDdY69uVBgmYV9GGoSFCpPpK2ckdUMA/viewform).
